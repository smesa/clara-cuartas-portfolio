About Me

About Me

Hello! I am Clara Cuartas, a graphic designer with over ten years of experience in UI/UX. I enjoy creating digital experiences that are both functional and accessible. With a strong background in psychology and teaching, I have a unique perspective on understanding and addressing user needs empathetically. My goal is to design solutions that truly make a difference in people's lives.

Professional Experience

Throughout my career, I have worked on designing interfaces for web and mobile devices, honing my UI and UX skills. My background in graphic design and constant updating on the latest UI/UX trends allow me to offer creative and effective solutions. I deeply appreciate teamwork and have led various projects, always aiming to create intuitive and accessible digital experiences. I strive to communicate clearly and guide teams to ensure user needs are always the priority.
